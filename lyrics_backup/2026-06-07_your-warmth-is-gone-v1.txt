[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can still feel the fire we started in the dark
Tracing the shadows that dance across your heart
Now the sheets are cold and the morning feels so long
I reach out for you but your warmth is gone

[Verse 1]
We used to tangle up beneath the fading light
Watching the sunset surrender to the night
Your fingertips were drawing rivers on my skin
A sweet surrender where we always let it in

[Pre-Chorus]
Every time the moonlight hits the wooden floor
I keep waiting by the open screen door
Craving the taste of secrets we would share
Breathing the wild sage lingering in the air

[Chorus]
Your warmth is gone but the craving stays alive
I need the touch that makes my spirit thrive
Pulling you closer in the spaces of my mind
The sweetest memory that I could ever find

[Post-Chorus]
Oh the embers are glowing but they need your flame
I am burning inside just whispering your name

[Verse 2]
I wander through the silent fields we used to know
Caught in the rhythm of an afterglow
I want the spark that happens when our eyes align
And taste the fever of your lips pressed into mine

[Pre-Chorus 2]
Every time the moonlight hits the wooden floor
I keep waiting by the open screen door
Craving the touch that takes my breath away
Holding the vision of you begging me to stay

[Chorus]
Your warmth is gone but the craving stays alive
I need the touch that makes my spirit thrive
Pulling you closer in the spaces of my mind
The sweetest memory that I could ever find

[Post-Chorus 2]
Oh the embers are glowing but they need your flame
I am burning inside just whispering your name

[Bridge]
The shadows are heavy and the night is so blind
Searching for the heaven that we left behind
Every drop of sweat was a promise that we made
Saeed will never let this burning passion fade
I want to hold you where the river softly bends
And ride this heavy longing till the bitter end

[Spoken]
(I still feel your heartbeat against mine)
(Come back and light this fire again)

[Final Chorus]
Your warmth is gone but the craving stays alive
I need the touch that makes my spirit thrive
Pulling you closer in the spaces of my mind
The sweetest memory that I could ever find
